WORKOUTS

Sixty

This total body workout pairs the Concept2 rower
with strength training in a high-intensity, high-energy
environment. Our motivational instructors
will lead you through every part of the workout in
our experiential light and sound studio. Sixty offers
a workout like no other with unmatchable
effectiveness and efficiency.

BARRE

Barre is a ballet inspired, calorie torching, music bumping,
total body workout for all fitness levels.
We use small isolated movements to create long,
lean muscles while incorporating cardio and
stretching to lift and tone your entire body.

ROW

A calorie-torching total body workout that builds
muscle, burns fat, and builds endurance all on a
single machine. With 30 and 45 minute class
lengths available, you can rack up the meters
and get your Sweat on while pushing yourself to
the finish.

LAB

Our LAB is your space to train how you'd like
outside of group fitness classes. We've stocked
the LAB with every piece of functional fitness
equipment we could think of, and then added
more for good measure. From Barbells to
dumbbells, Pelotons to Cable Machines, the LAB
has everything you need.
